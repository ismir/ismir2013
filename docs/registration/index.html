Registration

Registration Info

Online registration is now open. Registrations will be made through the  same system used for paper/music submission. Please, follow this link: https://www.softconf.com/d/ismir2013/ or click in the image below for registration. Once in the Softconf System, you should go to the “For Participants” to start your registration.

Remember that, for each paper/music piece, at least one author must be registered for the conference by August 19. If none of the authors is registered by this date, the paper/music piece will be removed from the conference proceedings and conference program.

 

Invitation Letter

If you need an invitation letter, after completing your registration and payment, send a request by e-mail to ismir2013brazil@gmail.com.

Proceedings

Electronic proceedings will be distributed at no extra cost to all registered participants. Printed proceedings will be provided, at the extra cost of USD 50 per book of proceedings, to participants who specifically ask for them in the registration process (think about the trees before you do). Notice the deadline for printed proceedings request. All participants who do not make this request at registration will not receive printed proceedings.

Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your registration by requesting a cancellation in writing to ismir2013brazil@gmail.com. In order to receive a refund, the request for cancellation must *be received* by ISMIR2013 Local Arrangements on or before 4 October 2013. If ISMIR2013 receives your written request for cancellation on or before 4 October 2013, you will be refunded your registration fee less the cancellation fee of $50 (USD). If your cancellation request is received on or after 5 October 2013, you will not receive a refund.
